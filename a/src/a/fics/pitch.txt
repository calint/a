    basic program for beginners and intermediate players   rating 1000 - 1600:
   1. play 5 blitz games first day at freechess.org    5 min
   2. use chess trainer to compose problems from your played games
   3. solve problems
   4. play one long game next day  30+ min
   5. repeat until reaching A class rating  ~1800
